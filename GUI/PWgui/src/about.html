About PWgui

PWgui is an input-file builder and a simple GUI for some programs
contained in the open-source Quantum ESPRESSO suite for quantum
simulation of materials (see http://www.quantum-espresso.org).

PWgui stands for PWscf gui, where PWscf is a component of Quantum ESPRESSO.
